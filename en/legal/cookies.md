# Cookie Policy

_The latest version of this document is always available at `https://useful.page.gd/cookie-policy`._

## 1. What are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help remember your preferences and improve performance.

## 2. Which Cookies We Use

- **Necessary** (language, theme, BVI) – always enabled.
- **Analytical** (Yandex.Metrika, Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity) – enabled only after your consent.
- **Functional** (interface settings) – also require consent.

## 3. How to Manage Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect site functionality. You can also set your consent via our cookie consent banner.

## 4. Storage Duration

Our cookies are stored for up to 365 days or until you delete them.

## 5. Third‑Party Cookies

Analytics services (Yandex, Google, Microsoft) may set their own cookies. We do not control them. See their policies for details.

## 6. Withdrawing Consent

You may withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies or resetting settings in the banner.

## 7. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here.

## 8. Contact

For cookie‑related questions: `privacy@useful.page.gd`.
