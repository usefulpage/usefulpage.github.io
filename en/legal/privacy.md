# Privacy Policy

_The latest version of this document is always available at `https://useful.page.gd/privacy-policy`._

## 1. What Data We Collect

We collect anonymous data about your actions on the site: pages viewed, tool usage, IP address (anonymised), browser type, and OS. With your consent (via the cookie banner), we also collect geolocation, browser fingerprint, and mouse movements.

## 2. How We Use Data

Data is used for:
- improving the site;
- analysing tool popularity;
- identifying errors and preventing abuse;
- debugging (administrator only).

## 3. Cookies

We use cookies to save your settings (language, theme, BVI mode). See the Cookie Policy for details.

## 4. Third‑Party Services

We use Yandex.Metrika, Google Analytics, and Microsoft Clarity for anonymous statistics. These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

## 5. Data Storage

Logs are stored on the server for 30 days, then automatically deleted. You may request deletion of your data by contacting `privacy@useful.page.gd`.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties, except as required by law.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

## 8. Children

The site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

## 9. Changes to Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page.

## 10. Contact

For any privacy‑related questions: `privacy@useful.page.gd`.
